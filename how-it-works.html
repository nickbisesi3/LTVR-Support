How It Works

Learning Time VR offers a comprehensive turnkey system to efficiently launch your school's VR program.

Headsets & Storage

We utilize Pico 4 Enterprise headsets, designed for learning with 4K displays, wide field of view, and long battery life. Each headset comes in its own case and is stored in portable containers with smart charging adapters for easy management and deployment between classrooms.

View Headset Specs

Headset Management

Your VR headsets arrive pre-installed with our management software. This allows us to manage applications, push updates, provide technical support, and track usage. Your team gets access to a dashboard to control content, monitor usage, and manage settings.

Curriculum & Content

Headsets are delivered ready-to-use with all subscribed applications pre-loaded. Students engage in asynchronous learning experiences, individually exploring virtual field trips, science labs, career simulations, and more.

We provide comprehensive training for your staff to ensure a smooth rollout and effective use of the VR platform. Ongoing support is available through our support portal and direct contact options.
