About LearningTime VR

Our Vision

Learning Time VR was created to provide revolutionary education to students around the world. Virtual Reality offers the opportunity to transport kids to new places, immerse them in a world of learning and play, and change the way they look at learning.

Our Mission

To empower learners of all ages with the transformative power of virtual reality. With our cutting-edge VR headsets, meticulously crafted content, and expert guidance, we are committed to reshaping the way knowledge is acquired and retained. Step into a world of endless possibilities where learning becomes a thrilling endeavor, and the boundaries of the traditional classroom fade away.

Accelerate Learning Growth

Immersive experiences boost understanding and speed up the learning process.

Increase Content Retention

Interactive VR learning leads to better short-term and long-term memory recall.

Provide Experiential Learning

Engage students with hands-on activities impossible in traditional settings.

Why Virtual Reality?

Experiential Learning

VR facilitates learning by doing, accelerating understanding and boosting retention beyond traditional methods.

Accelerated Retention

Remote Collaboration

Bridge geographical gaps by bringing students and educators together in shared virtual classrooms for learning and collaboration.

History of Learning Time

Learning Time began as three cherished early childhood education schools in Indianapolis, directed by Dr. Janice Bisesi. Following their closure due to the pandemic, Learning Time VR emerged to extend this educational ethos globally. Using immersive VR technology, we aim to ignite curiosity and learning in children worldwide, building on the original Learning Time legacy.
